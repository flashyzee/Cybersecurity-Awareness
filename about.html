More on Malware Types

  • Botnets: Networks of infected devices controlled by attackers, used for large-scale attacks.
  • Adware: Floods users with unwanted ads and tracks browsing habits for targeted advertising.
  • Viruses: Infect legitimate files or programs, spreading when infected files are shared.

How to Protect Yourself from Malware

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Add an extra layer of security to your accounts by using 2FA, which requires a second form of verification.
  • Keep Backups: Regularly backup important files in case of ransomware attacks or other malware threats.
  • Limit File Sharing and Access Permissions: Disable unnecessary file sharing and limit access permissions on shared devices to reduce exposure.
  • Beware of Phishing Scams: Be cautious of emails or messages requesting personal information. Verify senders before sharing sensitive data.
